Nice to meet you — I'm Hyojin Yang, a UX / Product Designer based in Stockholm.

I focus on building clear, intuitive experiences for complex systems—especially internal tools and platforms that are critical but often invisible. I care about making workflows feel simple, understandable, and trustworthy, without losing what actually matters.

I'm currently a Principal UX Designer at King, working closely with product and engineering to shape UX strategy, improve workflows, and help teams move from assumptions to shared understanding. I'm drawn to high-agency environments where problems are ambiguous, collaboration is strong, and design plays a strategic role in decision-making.

What keeps me excited about UX/Product Design is that the problems are always evolving. My work is grounded in listening closely, asking the right questions, and translating complexity into clarity—so people can do their best work with confidence.

Outside of day-to-day design work, I enjoy experimenting with new tools, building prototypes, and exploring how AI is reshaping how we design, think, and collaborate.
