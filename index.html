How I Work

A research-led loop, with AI accelerating every step that doesn't require human judgment.

01
Research Planning
Bouncing ideas and refining questions with AI before committing to a study design.
02
Insight Repository
Building living research repositories that stakeholders can explore and grow independently.
03
Stakeholder Alignment
Connecting research insights to roadmap priorities so teams build the right things.
04
AI Prototyping
Generating functional prototypes in hours to align teams on what we're building before committing.
05
Early User Testing
Testing with users on interactive prototypes before a single line of production code is written.
06
No-handoff Implementation
Using AI-assisted coding to ship production-quality interfaces under my review — no design-to-dev translation loss.

Throughout, I stay in control: AI handles the mechanical work; I handle the judgment calls.
